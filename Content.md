Version: 1.0

1. PROJECT IDENTITY & LORE

1.1 The Narrative

The Premise: The user is an Apprentice initiated into the "Guild of Form," an ancient order mastering the art of pulling geometry from the "Void." ZBrush is the "Grimoire."

The Antagonist: "Entropy." It manifests as bad topology and loss of momentum. It creeps in when the "Mana Flow" (Daily Streak) is broken.

The Goal: Ascend from Apprentice to Architect by mastering the tools and creating a "Masterpiece."

1.2 The Aesthetic

Tone: Dark Fantasy, Arcane, Professional.

Visuals: High-contrast. Deep slate backgrounds (The Void) with vibrant Amber/Gold accents (Magic/Fire).

üó∫Ô∏è PHASE 1: THE APPRENTICE'S TRIALS (FOUNDATION)

Strictly linear. All users must complete this to prove basic competency.

Chapter 1: Awakening the Eye
Goal: Surviving the UI and basic navigation.

Mission 1-1: The Blank Scroll

XP: 100

Scroll of Wisdom: "Without the ritual of 'Make PolyMesh3D', the clay remains dead stone. You cannot shape what has no soul. Press the Comma Key (,) to hide the Lightbox."

The Trial: Hide the Lightbox. Draw a Cylinder3D. Enter Edit Mode (T). Click 'Make PolyMesh3D'.

Mission 1-2: The Astral Projection

XP: 200

Scroll of Wisdom: "To shape the object, you must become the camera. Right-click empty space to Rotate. Alt+Right-click to Pan."

The Trial: Rotate 360 degrees. Pan to corner. Zoom in until it fills the screen.

Training Montage: The Dizzy Pilot

XP: 300

Drill: For 5 minutes: Top View (Snap Shift) -> Pan Bottom Right -> Frame (F) -> Side View -> Zoom In -> Frame. Repeat.

BOSS FIGHT: THE WATCHER

XP: 500

Test: Precision camera control.

Win Condition: Orient Cylinder to view Top Cap, Side, and Bottom perfectly flat using camera snaps.

Chapter 2: The Primordial Clay
Goal: Brush control and additive sculpting.

Mission 2-1: The Shapeless Form

XP: 250

Scroll of Wisdom: "Seek the silhouette. The Move spell (B-M-V) shifts mass without adding matter."

The Trial: Turn a sphere into a random lumpy potato shape using only Move.

Mission 2-2: Runes of Power

XP: 300

Scroll of Wisdom: "Clay Buildup (B-C-B) is the hammer. Add strips to build; hold Alt to carve."

The Trial: Sculpt the letter "A" on your potato. Use Alt to dig the hole.

Mission 2-3: The Banishing

XP: 200

Scroll of Wisdom: "Hold Shift to invoke the Banishing (Smooth). Overuse will erase details."

The Trial: Smooth the "A" until it disappears.

Training Montage: Scribble & Erase

XP: 400

Drill: Sculpt a letter. Smooth it away. Repeat for the whole alphabet.

BOSS FIGHT: THE TOTEM OF THREE

XP: 600

Test: Sculpt 3 distinct shapes on one sphere stack.

Win Condition: Square base, Round middle, Pyramid top. All from one sphere using Move, Clay, Smooth.

Chapter 3: The Eternal Forge
Goal: Dynamesh and Topology.

Mission 3-1: Stretching the Soul

XP: 250

Scroll of Wisdom: "See the geometry scream? This is 'Bad Topology'. You cannot carve runes into stretched soul-fabric."

The Trial: Pull a spike until polygons stretch and break.

Mission 3-2: The Rebirth Ritual

XP: 400

Scroll of Wisdom: "Invoke Dynamesh to redistribute matter. Ctrl+Drag in the void."

The Trial: Enable Dynamesh. Re-mesh the object. Smooth the spike.

Side Quest: Resolution Roulette

XP: 300

The Trial: Dynamesh at 32 Res vs 512 Res. Observe wireframe (Shift+F).

BOSS FIGHT: THE CHAOS SPIRE

XP: 750

Test: Abstract sculpture with twisting branches.

Win Condition: Pull branch -> Dynamesh -> Smooth -> Repeat. Mesh must remain clean.

Chapter 4: The Binding Spell
Goal: Masking.

Mission 4-1: The Shadow Glyph

XP: 300

Scroll of Wisdom: "Where shadow falls, clay is frozen. Hold Ctrl to paint Mask."

The Trial: Mask half a sphere. Deform the other half.

Mission 4-2: The Inversion

XP: 250

Scroll of Wisdom: "To shape the shadow, invert the world. Ctrl+Click background."

The Trial: Mask a spot. Invert. Pull out. Clear Mask (Ctrl+Drag BG).

Training Montage: The Tiger Ball

XP: 500

Drill: Mask stripe -> Invert -> Pull -> Clear. Repeat 20 times.

BOSS FIGHT: THE SPIRIT CHAIN

XP: 1000

Test: Create two interlinked rings from a single sphere.

Win Condition: Use masks to protect one ring while shaping the other.

Chapter 5: The Golem's Assembly
Goal: Subtools and Gizmo.

Mission 5-1: Conjuring Parts

XP: 350

Scroll of Wisdom: "A Golem is many pieces. Use 'Append' to summon new matter."

The Trial: Append a Cube and Cylinder.

Mission 5-2: Telekinesis

XP: 350

Scroll of Wisdom: "Press W for the Gizmo. Arrows move. Rings rotate. Squares scale."

The Trial: Pierce the cylinder through the cube using Gizmo.

Side Quest: The Wizard's Hat

XP: 250

The Trial: Append a Cone. Place it perfectly on top of the Sphere.

BOSS FIGHT: THE STONE GOLEM

XP: 1200

Test: Robot blockout using only primitives.

Win Condition: Head, Eyes, Neck, Body, Arms (10+ Subtools). Perfect placement.
